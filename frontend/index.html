AI Pipeline

How It Works

Six intelligent steps take your RFP from raw text to evaluated, priced proposal in minutes.

Monitor & Ingest Tenders

Admin publishes tenders with full descriptions and supporting documents. Applicants are notified of available opportunities.

1
2

Extract Requirements

The AI agent uses phi3:mini LLM to extract structured requirements from the tender's raw text description.

Match SKUs

FAISS vector embeddings semantically match extracted requirements to inventory SKUs with high accuracy.

3
4

Compute Pricing

A Python rules-based pricing engine dynamically calculates competitive offers per matched SKU.

Evaluate & Compare

AI consolidates all vendor proposals and produces a ranked comparison with the best applicant highlighted.

5
6

Offer & Accept

Admin sends targeted offers. Vendors accept or reject. Accepted contracts are stored and accessible to both parties.
